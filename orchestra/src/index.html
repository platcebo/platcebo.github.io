About us

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of legendary anime universes and the energy of the K-pop scene. Epic music, dynamic lighting design and the magic of a thousand candles create the aesthetics of a large-scale show. Music that you will recognize from the first notes.

Anime x K-pop Orchestra Experience is a large-scale symphonic event where iconic anime soundtracks meet the drive of global K-pop hits.

The virtuoso MUSE orchestra, powerful sound and scenic energy turn the concert into a vibrant event for fans of anime and K-pop.

The program features the most recognizable themes of cult universes in an orchestral interpretation. Before the concert, a cosplay festival awaits the audience - bright anime and K—pop images will become part of the atmosphere of the event.
