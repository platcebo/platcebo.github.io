HI!

The Enter team is here to help.

We are developing an HR-Tech product designed to support successful career starts for individuals and connect companies with new talent.

Enter for employees

We assist you in taking the first step in the digital environment. Discover various career opportunities in different companies, apply for internships, and create compelling resumes using our user-friendly visual editor.

Enter for employers

We are building a platform that facilitates effective recruitment of junior specialists in the digital sphere.

By enhancing the process of exploring career opportunities through company pages and an advanced job editor, we aim to reduce barriers and improve understanding between employers and candidates.
